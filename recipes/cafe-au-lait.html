Cafe Au Lait

>

Description

A cafe au lait is a drink made from brewed coffee and steamed milk. It's similar to a caffe latte, although it doesn't involve espresso.

Ingredients

  • Brewed coffee
  • Steamed milk

Steps

  1. Combine equal proportions of steamed milk and brewed coffee.
  2. (Optional) Dust with cinnamon powder.
